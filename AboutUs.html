About Us

Opened in the early 2000s, on the island of Saint Honorè, at the border between Macedonia and the Sahara dessert, Institute Ciak, si studia! It was born with the intention of introducing young people to the world of cinema and entertainment, preparing them to become the next shining stars of universal cinema. Several talents have emerged from our institution. Among the directors, we can count Pelo Ponneso, Bruno Liegi Bastonliegi, and Ugo Tiralaltro. As for actors and actresses, we have Pina Sinalefe, Herbert Ballerina, Rupert Sciamenna, Riccardino Fuffolo, and the multi-nominated Golden Goal recipient, Billy Ballo. Our students have also participated in various films, including “Giammangiato”, “Sossoldi”, “L'uomo che usciva la gente”, “Mani in alto”, “L'uomo che non reggeva l'alcol” and “Cento di questi Johnny”.
