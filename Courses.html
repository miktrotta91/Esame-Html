Directing

By Bruce Lagodigarda

The Directing course offers critical and operational tools that allow student directors to express and develop their own attitudes and to confront themselves with a professional and social reality in continuous evolution.

Screenwriting

By Manlio Cavaldonati

The course, organized by Ciak, si studia!, is aimed at all those who want to learn about the arts and techniques of film writing. During the course, students will engage in various individual and group practical exercises. The course develops over 330 hours.

Dubbing

By Salvo Errori

This course has been designed to train a complete professional in dubbing. The course is the result of over 20 years of experience and a methodology studied with the best professionals in the field. The course alternates theory and practice lessons, following the complete production process, from the idea to the finished product.

Acting

By Pino Cammino

This center offers a non-dogmatic acting course, which step by step, and through various methods of approach to the art of acting, offers a complete training and provides students with all the necessary tools to face work also in the theatrical and performative field.

Photography

By Colin Canon

This photography course aims to train the professional figure of the author of photography. During the three-year course, teaching modules related to the history of cinema and the analysis of the film work, the history of art and the technical and artistic deepening of the photographic shot, analog and digital, are active.

Cinematographic Makeup

By Anna Pannocchia

This course deepens the skills of the professional makeup artist, teaching how to create makeup adapted to the needs of cinema. The course is accessible to those who have the basics of makeup. The course prepares a makeup professional to be part of the cast within the film set and, in collaboration with other professionals, to create the character to be staged, according to the will of the director and what the screenplay requires.
